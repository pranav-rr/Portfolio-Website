About Me

Hello! My name is Pranav Ramesh and I’m a fourth year student studying Computer Science at the University of Michigan. I have a great passion for helping others, and I see Management Consulting as the primary pathway towards my pursuits.

This summer, I interned at an education benifits assistance company known as Guild Education. Their mission is to provide colligete opportunities for eligible workers at companies with big frontline populations. Examples include Walmart, Chipotle, Target, etc. During my time here, my big internship project was creating and organizing a reusable MBR Template for repeated discussions with my team's client: Disney. This template ended up having an even mix of trackable quantitative metrics (along with associated Excel visuals) and qualitative talking points. Outside of this project though, and on a more regular basis, I also aided team members with creating and organizing invoice charts, set up meetings with team members and non-team members alike to gain multiple perspectives on brainstormed ideas, and overall, became more aware with how client services consulting operated internally under a professional setting.

I've also applied myself towards other similar experiences prior to the role above. For example, due to the cancellation of my internship in the Summer of 2020, I took upon a Project Managing role at Ignis where I led an intercollegiate client services team of consultants to aid a care-packing company re-emerging out of Chicago, The Junk Bok. Our goal was to not only help them better understand their current/potential market, but to also introduce a result-driven social media strategy that they could utilize in the future. Later and during the Fall school semester, my team at Michigan Business Group and I worked with a local game studio, Nochi, to assess two main inquiries: whether or not R&D tax credit would be eligible for their company and whether they should go through with transitioning from an LLC to a S-Corp. We helped her make these decisions based on tax, shareholder, and internal implications and on the side, also helped her with more general processes.

More information on the above and other projects I've embarked on are included in my resume. At the bottom of this page is my contact info so feel free to send me a message if you have any questions or would simply like to know more. Enjoy your stay!
