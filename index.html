About Me

Hello! My name is Pranav Ramesh and I’m a third year student studying Computer Science with a minor in Multidisciplinary Design at the University of Michigan. I have a great passion for creativity, and I see both UI/UX and Consulting as pathways towards my pursuits.

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic and the cancellation of my internship, I took upon a Project Managing role at Ignis where I led an intercollegiate client service team of consults to aid with the re-emergence of a care-packing company based out of Chicago. Our goal was to not only help with providing a better understanding of their current/potential market, but to also introduce a passive social media strategy that they can utilize in the upcoming future. At the same time, I took it upon myself to familiarize myself with UI/UX practices by taking six courses that all led up to me completing a capstone project. This is attached below. At the bottom of this page is my contact info so feel free to send me a message if you have any questions or would like to know more about me. Enjoy your stay!
